Inspiro Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Inspiro (“we”, “our”, “us”) provides a tool that helps users search inspirational images and save selected items to their Pinterest boards.

Information We Collect

  • Pinterest OAuth tokens to authorize actions (create/read boards and pins).
  • Prompt text, selected image links/metadata (for creating Pins and logs).

How We Use Information

  • To connect your Pinterest account and perform requested actions (search, create Pins, ensure boards).
  • To improve reliability and prevent abuse (minimal logging such as timestamps and status).

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share personal information with third parties. Data is only sent to Pinterest APIs and (optionally) image providers such as Unsplash to fulfill your request.

Data Storage & Security

Access tokens are stored securely and rotated/revoked when possible. Logs contain minimal data (e.g., pin IDs, board names) and are retained only as needed for functionality.

Your Choices

  • You can revoke Pinterest access at any time via Pinterest account settings.
  • For deletion requests, contact us and we will remove associated data within a reasonable timeframe.
